The Malayan krait and the Lycodon are two species of snakes that have very similar appearances, which can easily confuse many people and make it difficult to distinguish between them. The dangerous difference between them is that the Malayan krait is venomous, with its venom being ranked the deadliest in Thailand. If bitten, the chance of death is as high as 90%. On the other hand, the Lycodon is non-venomous. Therefore, it is important to be able to distinguish between these two species for your own safety and the safety of others.

CV, or Computer Vision, is an open-source library widely used for image and video processing. It allows us to read images, resize them, extract individual color channels, and further use them in Deep Learning applications.
